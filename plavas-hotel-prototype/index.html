Location

Coastal Ainaži

Restaurant Pļavas is located in Ainaži, where the air is fragrant with pine trees and the sea is just a five-minute walk away. Enjoy a beautiful view of the well-kept garden from a table in the restaurant or on the summer patio.

The holiday house accommodation is also just a 5-minute walk from the beach, with hiking trails to explore the beauty of coastal nature.

  • Address Valdemāra iela 121, Ainaži, Latvija
  • Beach 5-minute walk
